Draft with ChatGPT

Select a placeholder in your email (e.g., “help draft: confirm budget and thank the team”), then click Draft with ChatGPT on the ribbon.

  • Works in Compose (new/reply/forward) on Outlook web or desktop.
  • The add-in sends your placeholder text to your backend and replaces it with AI output.

Tip: Keep prompts short and specific. You can include tone hints like “warm, concise”.
